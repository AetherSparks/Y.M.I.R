Cookie Policy

Effective Date: March 18, 2025

1. Introduction

At EmotionAI, we use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them specifically for our AI-based emotion detection and music recommendation services, and how you can control them.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us recognize your device, remember your preferences, and gather analytical data to improve your experience with our emotion detection and music recommendation services.

3. How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for basic website functionality, security, and to enable our AI-based emotion detection and music recommendation features.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our site (e.g., Google Analytics) and improve our AI algorithms.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences, settings, and emotion detection permissions.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads and measure their effectiveness.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics, social media plugins, or embedded media players that set cookies on your device. These services help us provide our emotion detection and music recommendation features. These third-party cookies are governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Special Purpose Cookies

  • Emotion Detection Cookies: Store temporary data about your emotion states detected via webcam or text interactions.
  • Music Recommendation Cookies: Remember your music preferences and listening history to improve recommendations.
  • Chatbot Interaction Cookies: Store conversation history for better contextual responses.

6. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings or our cookie settings panel. Please note, disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality and limit our ability to provide personalized emotion detection and music recommendations. Here's how to manage cookies on popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
