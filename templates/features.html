Key Features

Facial Emotion Recognition

Our system continuously detects and analyzes your facial expressions via webcam to understand your current emotional state in real-time.

AI Chatbot

Interact with our intelligent chatbot that detects emotions from your text and analyzes conversation history to better understand your mood.

Emotion Logging

We track and log your emotions from both facial recognition and text analysis, averaging them to determine your dominant emotional state.

How It Works

1

Emotion Detection

Our system uses your webcam to analyze facial expressions and detects emotions from your chat interactions. These inputs are processed in real-time using advanced AI algorithms.

2

Emotion Analysis

We combine and average the emotions detected from your face and text to determine your dominant emotional state. This data is securely stored to improve future recommendations.

3

Music Selection

Our Content-Based Filtering model analyzes your emotional state and matches it with songs from our database based on mood labels, mental health benefits, and musical features.

4

Playback

The system fetches the recommended songs and plays them in our custom mini-player. The audio plays seamlessly in the background without showing external player interfaces.
