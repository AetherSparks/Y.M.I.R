Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our emotion detection technology.

How accurate is the emotion detection?

Our system achieves 98.7% accuracy in controlled environments and over 92% accuracy in varied lighting conditions. The AI continually improves through federated learning while maintaining strict privacy standards.

Is my facial data stored or shared?

No. We process all facial data locally in your browser. No images or facial recognition data are stored on our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security at all times.

What emotions can the system detect?

Our system detects seven primary emotions: happiness, sadness, anger, fear, surprise, disgust, and neutral, with additional capability to recognize complex emotional states like contemplation and confusion.

Can I use this on mobile devices?

Yes. Our platform is fully responsive and optimized for both desktop and mobile devices. The mobile version uses specialized lightweight models for efficient processing without compromising accuracy.
